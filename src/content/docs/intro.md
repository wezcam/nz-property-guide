Welcome to the ultimate resource for Kiwi homeowners looking to bypass traditional real estate agent fees. On an average \$1,000,000 NZD home, traditional tiered commissions plus GST can strip up to **\$30,000** of your hard-earned equity. 

This completely free, step-by-step framework will guide you safely through the entire legal, marketing, and sales process used by professionals.

## Core Guide Pathways

*   **1. Preparation & Pricing:** Learn how to professionally stage your home, utilize modern AI to extract stunning listing photos from video, and establish a bulletproof pricing strategy.
*   **2. Legal & Paperwork:** Demystify the standard REINZ/ADLS Sale and Purchase Agreement and learn exactly how to coordinate with your NZ conveyancing solicitor.
