Welcome to the ultimate operational manual for selling your home privately in New Zealand. 

The traditional real estate model dictates that you must hand over tens of thousands of dollars in hard-earned equity to an agency just to list a home on the internet. In the modern, connected Kiwi market, that is simply no longer true. With over 90% of buyers tracking the market directly on Trade Me Property, you have the exact same reach as a commercial agency.

This guide is designed to remove the mystery, lower the stress, and give you a step-by-step roadmap to handle your own listing safely and professionally.

---

## What This Manual Covers

This guide is structured into logical, actionable operational phases to take you from an initial concept to a unconditional cash settlement:

*   **Phase 1: Preparation & Marketing:** Organizing your pre-prepared contract packs, securing a council LIM report early, setting a data-driven price bracket, and executing a secure open home strategy.
*   **Phase 2: Legal & Paperwork:** Navigating the standard ADLS/REINZ Sale and Purchase Agreement, managing multi-offer scenarios, and working effectively with your property lawyer.

---

## Your Core Project Team

Even though you are selling without an agent, you are never truly flying completely solo. Your private sale ecosystem relies on three core local professionals:

1.  **Your Conveyancing Solicitor:** The absolute anchor of your transaction. They manage the legal title transfer and hold the deposit in a secure trust account.
2.  **A Registered Property Valuer:** Optional, but highly recommended if you want an unbiased, rock-solid appraisal to show banks and hesitant buyers.
3.  **A Professional Photographer:** High-quality imagery and accurate digital floor plans are non-negotiable for making your digital listing stand out from the crowd.
